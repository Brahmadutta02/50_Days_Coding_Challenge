Welcome to
MAA’nka Chunka

Welcome to MAA’nka Chunka, where authentic flavors meet exceptional dining experiences. Nestled in the heart of Balasore, our restaurant takes pride in offering you an unforgettable culinary journey through the rich and diverse tapestry of Indian cuisine.

Step into our warm and inviting ambiance, where the aroma of aromatic spices fills the air and the vibrant colors of Indian decor transport you to the magical land of India. Our mission is to provide you with an authentic taste of India, prepared with utmost care and passion by our talented team of chefs.

Come and embark on a culinary adventure at MAA’nka Chunka, where the traditions and flavors of India come alive. We look forward to serving you and sharing the authentic taste of India with you.
